- Main Characters -

Buffy Summers

Buffy Summers, is the titular character, a high school student who discovers she is the Slayer, (portrayed by Sarah Michelle Gellar) and the chosen one, destined to battle vampires, demons, and other forces of darkness.

Willow Rosenberg

Willow is Buffy’s loyal friend and a powerful witch. Over the course of the series, she transforms from a shy computer nerd into an incredibly strong witch.

Xander Harris

Xander brings humor and heart to the group. Though not supernaturally gifted, his courage and loyalty make him an invaluable member of the “Scooby Gang.”

Rupert Giles

Giles is Buffy’s Watcher, mentor, and father figure. He offers guidance, knowledge, and an extensive library of magical texts to help fight supernatural threats.

Angel

Angel (portrayed by David Boreanaz) is a vampire cursed with a soul, struggling to atone for his dark past by helping Buffy and the gang fight evil forces.

Spike

Spike (portrayed by James Marsters) starts off as a villainous vampire but evolves into a fan-favorite antihero. His sharp wit and fighting prowess are invaluable in the battle against darkness.

Anya Jenkins

Formerly a vengeance demon specializing in punishing unfaithful men, Anya eventually becomes human. She struggles with humanity’s nuances but provides candid (often hilarious) insights.

Tara Maclay

Tara is a gentle, introspective witch who becomes Willow’s partner and an important member of the Scooby Gang. Her empathy and magical abilities complement Willow’s talents.
