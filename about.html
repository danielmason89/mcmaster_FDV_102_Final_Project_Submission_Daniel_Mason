- About the Creator -

"Buffy the Vampire Slayer" was created by Joss Whedon. It first aired in 1997 and quickly became a cult classic, influencing pop culture for years to come.

Joss Whedon

Joss Whedon is an American filmmaker known for creating strong female characters and innovative storytelling. Beyond "Buffy," he has been involved in projects like "Angel," "Firefly," and many more.
